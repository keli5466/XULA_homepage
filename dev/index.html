This is XULA.

Tomorrow’s leaders walk our campus today. Will you walk among them?



Standing in a city rich with history, Xavier University of Louisiana has been poised to contribute to New Orleans’ impressive roster of movers and shakers since its inception in 1915.


As the country’s only Catholic and Historically Black College or University, XULA is in a unique position to graduate global citizens on our pillars of faith, service and community.


Dedicated to meeting students where they are and nurturing them towards their truest potential, Xavier’s passionate and devoted educators help each incoming class leave ready to make their mark on this world.
